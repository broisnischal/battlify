Battery History now tracks **lid-closed sessions** — it records the charge when
the lid closes and when it reopens, and shows how much drained (and the %/hour
rate) while the Mac was closed. Also improved Wi-Fi/Bluetooth restore-on-wake
(delay + retry until the hardware is ready) and faster post-wake refresh of the
battery, charge, and lid state.
